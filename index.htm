Weapons

Uzi-gun

Shoots five shots automatically one after the other.

Shotgun

Three shots are simultaneously fired with a wide range

Bazooka

This will be affected by the wind and power. Hold the spacebar for more power.

The Holy Grenade

Bounces around until it stop and explodes. Affected by power of throw, but is heavier so it won't be affected as much by the wind as the Bazooka. It bounces eight times before it explodes

Grenade

Has a three second timer, and provides less damage than the Holy Grenade.

Airstrike

Shoots five missles from an airplane at the top of the screen. Use the mouse to specify where it will drop the bombs.

Teleport

Align your mouse to the place you'd like to teleport to and press space. Be careful what you wish for

Baseball bat

Put on your baseball cap and take a swip at your opponent

Blowtorch

Insanely overpowered fireball that will delete any map, and burn any snail in it's way.
